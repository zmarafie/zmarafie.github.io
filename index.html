Welcome to the AI and IoT Research Lab

Advancing the frontiers of Artificial Intelligence and the Internet of Things through innovation and collaboration.

About Us

The AI and IoT Research Lab, led by Dr. Zahraa Marafie, focuses on integrating cutting-edge technologies to solve real-world challenges. With a multidisciplinary team and partnerships across academia and industry, the lab is at the forefront of technological advancements in AI and IoT.

Our key projects include:

  • HeatWatch: Real-time heat exhaustion detection
  • CarPark: A smart parking assistant application
  • Business Data Analysis: Optimizing real estate operations

Theory and Frameworks

The lab develops theoretical models and frameworks to push the boundaries of AI and IoT technologies. Highlights include:

  • Personality-based service models for IoT agents
  • Edge and fog intelligence frameworks
  • Real-time systems optimization
