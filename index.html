About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Science at Kuwait University, holding both a Master's and a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the University of California, Irvine. My expertise lies in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and the Internet of Things (IoT), with a focus on Real-Time Systems and Service-Oriented Computing.

During my doctoral research, I designed and implemented the "AutoCoach" system, an innovative solution that leverages AI and IoT technologies to analyze and personalize driver behavior feedback, enhancing road safety through tailored alerts and analytics. Currently, my research explores practical AI and IoT applications, including HeatWatch, a real-time heat exhaustion detection system; CarPark, a smart parking assistant application; and Business Data Analysis to optimize real estate operations.

Beyond my academic and research pursuits, I proudly supervise the ClickIT Club at the ISC Department, fostering innovation and collaboration among students in information systems and computing.
